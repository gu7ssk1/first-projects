Richard B. Riddick

The one who survived

History

  • The necromonger Zhylaw recives a profacy that tells of the downfall of his kingdom by a Furyan male.
  • Zhylaw, now the Lord Marshall, massacress all the Furyan babies by strangling them with their umbelical chords. Riddick is one of the only surviovors, left for dead.
  • Later in life, Riddick repressed his memories, believeing his mother tried to strangle him and left him behind a liquor store.
  • Riddick spends most of his childhood in juvy and later in prison.
  • Following a murder charge and several escape attempts, Riddick is sent to Bucher's Bay- a maxmimum security in-escapeable prison.
  • During his time in prison, Riddick gets his infamous 'eyeshine'.
  • After escaping Butcher's Bay, Riddick is captured by the bounty hunter Johns. on their way to a penal colony they crash on an unkonwn planet with 3 suns.
  • Riddick escapes to the planet UV. there he is attacked by Necromongers, and eventually he avenges them by overthrowing their Lord Marshall and taking his position.
  • Riddick goes on a quest in search of old furya, but ends up on a desolate dangerous planet.
  • Ironically, Riddick escapes the planet with the aid of his former enemy John's father.
  • Riddick carries on in his quest in search of his self and his ancestorial home Furya
